Violet Rose and Vector

Violet Rose is a mysterious superhero who blends the beauty of nature with the power of darkness. With deep purple energy flowing through their veins, they use their abilities to protect the innocent.

Vector, the trusted sidekick, helps navigate the team's missions with his quick reflexes and intelligence.

Powers:

  • Violet Rose
    1. Can manipulate plant life to create barriers and traps.
    2. Uses energy blasts infused with violet light.
  • Vector
    1. Has the ability to camouflage into any environment.
